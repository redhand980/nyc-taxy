FAQ

Answering Your Questions

If you have any questions about our taxi service, please contact us in any convenient way,
and also read the information below.

How to book a ride?

You can book a ride by filling out the form on our website. In this case, we will contact you to confirm your request. Or you can download our app.

Where can I download your app?

The NYC Taxi app is available for iOS and Android smartphones. Download it from Google Play and App Store.

Do you provide transfer services from the airport?

Yes, we can pick you up at the LaGuardia Airport, John F. Kennedy International Airport, and Newark Liberty International Airport.

Can I book a ride early in the morning?

Sure. you can book a ride whenever you need. We work 24/7

How many drivers do you have?

More than 700 drivers are connected to our system. And we are constantly growing!

Why do you have a fee for booking in advance?

By paying the fee for booking in advance, you can be sure that on the specified date and time, the cab will be there.

Can I call a taxi by phone?

Yes, call +1 (234) 567 89 00. But are operators can be busy, we proceed online requests faster.

Can I cancel my ride?

Yes, you can cancel your request but only before our cab arrives.
